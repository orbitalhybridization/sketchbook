About Me

Hello, I'm Ian Jackson

I’m a second-year PhD student in Cognitive Science, where my research focuses on cognitive neuroscience and neurotechnology. My work explores how complex systems and dynamical processes in the brain give rise to cognition and behavior, and how these systems can be modeled computationally. As a passionate advocate for the intersection of science and technology, I strive to push the boundaries of understanding how the brain works and how we can leverage this to create novel outputs into the world.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I’m deeply interested in the creative side of life. This website serves as a platform to showcase my academic work while also providing space for my creative endeavors. From music creation to casual explorations in video games, this is where I can merge my technical and artistic passions. Whether I’m crafting a new song, tossing a frisbee, or testing out a new recipe in the kitchen, I find inspiration in the balance of intellectual exploration and creative expression.
