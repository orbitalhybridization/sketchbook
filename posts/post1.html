noisepoetry

Published Dec 24, 2024

Edited Dec 24, 2024

This is my first "completed" project in Ableton. I've had a strong affinity in the last several years for the noise and experimental scene, and have especially liked the contrast of harsh noise against calmer vocals. I recorded a journal entry of mine and thought it would sound nice against that very backdrop. Added few simple chords to give it some more color (albeit a melancholy one). This is just a little draft, a creative exercise to put music into practice.

The sound is strongly influenced by Lingua Ignota's 2021 EP Agnus Dei. Kristin Hayter always has a way of finding that perfect balance between serenity and madness, and I wanted to try my hand at expressing that in my own way.
