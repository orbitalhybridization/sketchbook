Oh, happiness! (Song 89)

Published Jan 18, 2025

This folksy tune came to me during one of the nightly creative practice sessions I've been having over the last few months. The sound is primarily inspired by Adrianne Lenker, Haley Heynderickx, and a bit of Angel Olsen. I really like the lilting harmonies and the dissonant B6 contrasted against the E Major.

As I was putting the song together, it started to feel like one of those folk songs that tells a story. The song Lighthouse by Adrianne Lenker from Hours Were the Birds especially came to mind (where she also sings about the woes of being seventeen). The story is a fun little fabrication, and he working title of the song (Oh, happiness!) is a bit of a play on the Molly Drake song, Happiness, from The Tide's Magnificence: Songs and Poems of Molly Drake.
